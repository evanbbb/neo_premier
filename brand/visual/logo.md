# Logo

The Neo Premier League primary mark. Asset id **`npl-logo`** (on-image/file label `npl_logo.png`).

## What it is

A black-and-white **shield crest**:

- A **soccer ball** at the centre.
- **Circuit-trace lines** radiating behind the ball — the engineering/"circuits" signal.
- A **banner** across the lower third reading **NPL**.
- **EST.2099** beneath the banner.

Greyscale, high-contrast, badge-like — it reads as a football club crest built by engineers, which
is exactly the brand ([`../foundation.md`](../foundation.md)).

## Usage

- **Greyscale only.** Black on light, or reversed to white on dark. Never recolour it
  ([`color.md`](color.md)).
- **Clear space.** Keep a margin of empty space around the crest equal to roughly the height of the
  NPL banner; don't crowd it with type or graphics.
- **Minimum size.** Big enough that "NPL" and "EST.2099" stay legible; if they'd disappear, use a
  simplified placement (crest only) or size up.
- **Don't** distort, rotate, recolour, add effects to, or rebuild the crest. Place the supplied
  asset; don't regenerate it.
- **Pairs with** British Inserat headlines and the shape/overlay system ([`identity.md`](identity.md)).

## Where it's used

- The mark for the league: profiles, broadcast bug, merch, and as a lockup on key-art (placed by
  [`../../skills/generation/key-art-composer/SKILL.md`](../../skills/generation/key-art-composer/SKILL.md)).
- It is a **finished asset** — attach `npl-logo` and place it; image generators should **not** try
  to draw it (the underlying image stays logo-free; the logo goes on in composition).
