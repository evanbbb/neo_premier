# Billboard / OOH

**Purpose:** Out-of-home — read in a glance, at distance, at speed.

**Length:** 3–7 words. One idea, no second thought.

**Register:** Bold, declarative, instantly legible. Built for impact at 70mph.

**Format:** A single dominant line, set huge in British Inserat
([`../visual/typography.md`](../visual/typography.md)). No supporting paragraph.

**Do:** Pick the most visceral angle and commit. High contrast, one thought.

**Don't:** Try to say two things. Use small print to "explain" the line. Go corporate.

**Example:** *Two titans. One crown.*
