# Jersey / Kit

**Purpose:** Worn copy — a team motto, a crest line, something fans put on their bodies.

**Length:** 1–4 words. Wearable means short.

**Register:** Iconic, tribal, motto-like. A line you'd be proud to wear into the arena.

**Format:** Usually UPPERCASE, British Inserat. Reads as a badge, not a sentence.

**Do:** Make it ownable and a little dangerous. Tribal identity over description.

**Don't:** Be cute or novelty ([`../anti-patterns.md`](../anti-patterns.md)). Use punctuation-heavy
phrasing.

**Example:** *BUILT TO CLASH*
