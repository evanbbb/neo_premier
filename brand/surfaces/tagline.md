# Tagline / Headline

**Purpose:** A single, iconic line — the kind that could live on a title card, a jersey, or an
arena banner.

**Length:** 10 words or fewer. Shorter is stronger.

**Register:** Maximum compression. Cinematic, declarative, memorable. Every word load-bearing.

**Format:** One line. May use a two- or three-beat structure for rhythm
("Wires will tear. Sparks will fly. Crowns will be claimed.").

**Do:** Land one idea. Be broadcast-ready. Foreground the clash, the builders, or the moment.

**Don't:** Explain. Stack clauses. Use banned/corporate words ([`../lexicon.md`](../lexicon.md)).

**Example:** *Built by Humans. Played by Machines.*
