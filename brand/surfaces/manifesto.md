# Manifesto / Announcement

**Purpose:** The myth of the league — a founding statement, a season opener, a big announcement.

**Length:** 1–3 paragraphs.

**Register:** Epic and declarative. The voice of a sport announcing itself to the world.

**Format:** Long-form. Build a world: where it came from, what's at stake, why it matters. End on a
line that could be carved over the arena door. Return a single strong piece (not a list).

**Do:** Tell the origin and the stakes. Foreground the builders. Earn every big claim with a
concrete image. Ladder to a [messaging pillar](../messaging.md).

**Don't:** Drift into press-release neutrality. Pad with abstractions. List features.

**Example:** See the long boilerplate in [`../messaging.md`](../messaging.md), heightened — the
movement born from championship robotics teams, taking machines from the lab to the arena.
