# Teaser

**Purpose:** Build anticipation for a moment to come — a launch, a reveal, a match.

**Length:** 3–5 sentences with a rising rhythm.

**Register:** Cinematic build. Quiet-to-loud. Ends on a challenge or a question.

**Format:** Short escalating lines that crescendo, then a turn ("This is not the start of a
season…") and a final beat that dares the reader in.

**Do:** Build stakes. Use concrete, visceral images. Reframe the moment as historic.

**Don't:** Give everything away. Flatten into "coming soon." Resolve the tension too early.

**Example:**
The silence before the roar. The hum before the thunder. From workshops the world never watched,
the builders have unleashed their titans. This is not the start of a season — it is the ignition
of a sport. Are you ready to witness history?
