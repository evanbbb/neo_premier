# Social Post

**Purpose:** A post for feed/timeline — built to stop the scroll and get shared.

**Length:** 1–3 sentences. The first line is the hook and must stand alone.

**Register:** Conversational but electric. Native to the platform, never an ad read.

**Format:** Hook line first, then a beat of payoff. Optional sign-off line. Hashtags only if they
earn their place — never filler.

**Do:** Open with the most charged image. Write the first line to survive on its own.

**Don't:** Bury the hook. Use "don't miss out" / "tune in" filler ([`../lexicon.md`](../lexicon.md)).

**Example:**
A robot just scored a bicycle kick in front of 40,000 people. Your move, football.
The Neo Premier League is here.
