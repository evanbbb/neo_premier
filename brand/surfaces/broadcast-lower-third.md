# Broadcast Lower-Third

**Purpose:** On-screen text under live action — chyrons, score bugs, moment callouts.

**Length:** A phrase, ~2–6 words. Reads in a beat without pulling focus from the play.

**Register:** Punchy, present-tense, high-energy. Commentator-adjacent.

**Format:** A short phrase or label. Often paired with names/scores. Mono telemetry style for
data tags ([`../visual/typography.md`](../visual/typography.md)).

**Do:** Use live, present-tense energy. Make it callable out loud.

**Don't:** Write a sentence. Bury the moment in words. Compete with the action.

**Example:** *BICYCLE KICK — REPLAY*
