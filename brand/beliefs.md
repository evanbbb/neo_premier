# Beliefs

What the Neo Premier League is convinced of. Beliefs are deeper than taglines — they are the
convictions every piece of work should be able to trace back to. When a decision is hard, return
here.

## 1. The future is humans building machines that play

Not humans versus machines. The drama is human ambition expressed through steel. Every robot on
the pitch is a human team's will made physical.

## 2. Engineering is athletic performance

A clutch repair, a bold design gamble, a build that survives a brutal season — these are athletic
feats. We treat invention with the reverence sport reserves for talent.

## 3. Failure is part of the show

Machines break. Spectacularly. We do not hide it — we frame it. A robot that fails, gets rebuilt,
and returns is the oldest sports story there is, told in a new language.

## 4. Spectacle earns the science

The crowd, the lights, the rivalry — these are not decoration on top of "serious" robotics. The
spectacle is how robotics becomes a sport the world actually watches. We refuse the false choice
between rigorous and thrilling.

## 5. Real stakes or it doesn't count

A league, a season, a crown. Rankings that hurt to lose. Without genuine competitive stakes, it's
a demo. We build stakes into everything.

## 6. Builders are heroes

The engineers are not backstage staff — they are athletes and protagonists. We put the makers in
the frame, not just the machines.

## 7. Chaos is a feature

Sparks, collisions, the unscripted moment no one can predict. We design for emergence, not for a
sanitized, on-rails experience.

## 8. Precision is the discipline beneath the chaos

The chaos lands because the craft underneath is exact. Tight engineering, tight design, tight
broadcast. We earn the right to be wild by being precise.

## 9. This is a beginning, not a product launch

We talk like the first chapter of a sport's history is being written — because it is. Never the
language of a feature drop or a quarterly release.

## 10. Optimism with teeth

We believe in a bright, invented future — and we make it feel hungry, competitive, and earned,
never soft or utopian.

## How beliefs are used

- They are the deepest test in [`copy-reviewer`](../skills/copy-reviewer/SKILL.md) and
  [`image-reviewer`](../skills/image-reviewer/SKILL.md): does the work express a belief, or just
  decorate one?
- When the brand changes direction, [`brand-evolver`](../skills/brand-evolver/SKILL.md) should
  reconcile the change against these beliefs and log it in [`decisions/`](decisions/).
