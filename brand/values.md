# Values

How the Neo Premier League behaves. Beliefs are what we think; values are how we act — the
operating principles behind decisions, partnerships, and craft.

## Build in the open

Show the work. The grit, the prototypes, the failures, the rebuilds. Polish hides the human story
that makes this a sport. Transparency is more compelling than perfection.

## Earn the spectacle

Big moments are paid for with real engineering and real stakes. We never fake the drama. Hype is
a promise — we keep it on the pitch.

## Respect the maker and the fan equally

The hardcore builder and the first-time spectator both belong here. We never talk down to the
expert or lock out the newcomer (see [`audience.md`](audience.md)).

## Precision in the craft, chaos in the show

Sloppy work, sharp show? No. The discipline is invisible and total; the spectacle is loud and
alive. One serves the other.

## Compete with honor, win with swagger

Rivalry is sacred. We celebrate hard-fought wins and respect the defeated. Trash talk is theater,
not cruelty.

## Bias toward bold

When choosing between safe and striking, choose striking — then make it work. Timidity is the one
unforgivable move for a brand inventing a sport.

## Human-made, always

Machines play; humans build and decide. We never blur that line into "the robots took over." The
credit, and the heart, belongs to people.
