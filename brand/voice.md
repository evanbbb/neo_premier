# Voice & Tone

How the Neo Premier League sounds. This is the primary input for every text skill. Pair it with
[`lexicon.md`](lexicon.md) (specific words) and [`anti-patterns.md`](anti-patterns.md) (what to
avoid).

## Voice in one line

We sound like a broadcast on the night a new sport is born: electric, cinematic, precise, and
hungry — made by human hands.

## Tone keywords

Electric · inventive · bold · competitive · optimistic · engineered · chaotic · cinematic ·
precise · human-made · future-facing · visceral · heroic · legendary · hungry · unscripted ·
stadium-shaking · gladiatorial · triumphant.

## Voice principles

1. **Make it feel historic.** The audience should feel they're witnessing something for the first
   time. Something monumental is *beginning* — never "starting soon."
2. **Stakes, rivalry, heroism.** Build emotional investment through the clash of titans, not
   feature lists.
3. **Visceral over abstract.** Sparks, steel, the roar of the crowd — concrete physical images,
   not vague futurism.
4. **Precise, not flowery.** Short, engineered sentences. Every word load-bearing. Cinematic ≠
   wordy.
5. **Human-made, foregrounded.** Credit the builders. The heart is human even when the players are
   machines.
6. **Confidence, not arrogance.** We've earned the swagger; we don't sneer.

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| "The ignition of a new era." | "Starts soon." / "Now live." |
| "Wires will tear. Sparks will fly." | "Experience next-gen robotics." |
| "Built by humans. Played by machines." | "The future of sport is here." |
| Name the stakes, the rivalry, the moment | Lead with specs or features |
| Punchy, broadcast-ready lines | Corporate hype clichés |
| Show grit and failure | Sanitize into a glossy demo |

## Register by surface

The voice is constant; the **register** shifts with the surface. Skills should pick the register
that matches the requested surface.

| Surface | Register | Length | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tagline / headline | Maximum compression, cinematic | ≤ 10 words | Could live on a jersey or title card. |
| OOH / billboard | Bold, declarative, instantly legible | 3–7 words | Read at 70mph. One idea. |
| Jersey / kit | Iconic, motto-like | 1–4 words | Wearable. Tribal. |
| Broadcast lower-third | Punchy, present-tense, high-energy | A phrase | Reads under live action. |
| Social post | Conversational-but-electric, hooky | 1–3 sentences | First line is the hook. |
| Teaser | Building anticipation, rising rhythm | 3–5 sentences | Ends on a challenge or question. |
| Manifesto / announcement | Epic, declarative, high-stakes | 1–3 paragraphs | The myth of the league. |
| Press release | Credible + electric; facts that still feel like a moment | Standard PR shape | Never goes flat or corporate. |

## Reference outputs (the bar)

- **Teaser:** "The silence before the roar. The hum before the thunder. From labs hidden from the
  world, the engineers have unleashed their titans — steel gladiators built not to serve, but to
  conquer. This is not the start of a season. This is the ignition of a new era. The Neo Premier
  League begins. Are you ready to witness history?"
- **Championship tagline:** "Wires will tear. Sparks will fly. Crowns will be claimed."
- **Brand tagline:** "Built by Humans. Played by Machines."

## Reference points (vibe, not literal)

League of Legends energy · NASA ambition · garage-built grit · Formula 1 engineering drama ·
robot-sport spectacle. See [`anti-patterns.md`](anti-patterns.md) for the energies we avoid.
